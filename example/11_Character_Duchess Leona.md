Duchess Leona Ashford controls the southern provinces and commands the largest
private army in the realm. She was once Aldric's closest ally but has turned
against him — and the crown — for reasons she keeps carefully hidden.

**Secret:** Her eldest son was killed by a previous crown bearer. She believes
the Shattered Crown is a curse, not a blessing, and she will destroy every shard
rather than let another bearer rise.

**Appearance:** Tall, always impeccably dressed, with rings on every finger. Each
ring is from a different province — a political statement.
