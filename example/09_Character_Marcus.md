Marcus Vael is a former soldier turned woodcarver. He deserted from the King's
Guard after refusing an order to burn a village. He carries the guilt of that
day — not for deserting, but for not deserting sooner.

He met Anna on the road to Thornfield and has stayed by her side since. He
claims it's because she's the only person who doesn't ask him about his past.

**Key trait:** Loyal to a fault. Will fight anyone for Anna but won't admit why.
