A network of volcanic caves beneath the Shattered Peaks. The name is ancient —
predating any living memory — and no one is sure who or what "Voldemort" originally
referred to.

The caves are used as a neutral meeting ground by those who want to conduct
business away from prying eyes. The sulfur smell never quite washes out of your
clothes.
