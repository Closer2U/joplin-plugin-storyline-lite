Lord Aldric Vane is the aging warden of Thornfield Castle. He has protected the
northern border for three decades and is one of the few people alive who remembers
the original Shattered Crown.

He believes the crown must be reforged — but his methods are increasingly
ruthless. He sees Anna as a tool to be used, though he genuinely cares for her
in his own rigid way.

**Conflict:** His sense of duty overrides his sense of morality. He will sacrifice
anyone for the realm — including himself.

**Note:** Shares the alias "The Commander" with Marcus. This tests disambiguation.
