```
Thornfield Castle sits on a granite bluff overlooking the Greymist Valley. It was
built during the First Age as a frontier fortress and has been expanded many times
since.

**Key areas:**
- The Great Hall — where the council meets
- The Gallery — a hidden balcony above the hall
- The Gardens — an unexpectedly peaceful courtyard with an ancient oak
- The Outer Wall — the main defensive perimeter with warning bells

**Atmosphere:** Cold stone, drafty corridors, surprisingly good food. The castle
has a personality — it creaks and groans as if it has opinions about its occupants.
```
