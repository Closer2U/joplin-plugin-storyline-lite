Aldric followed the secret passage beneath the mountain. He hadn't been to
Voldemort's Lair since the old wars, and the air still tasted of sulfur and regret.

Duchess Leona was already there, standing in the firelight. Beside her stood a
figure in black armor — one of the Crown Seekers.

"You were right to come alone," Leona said, though her voice wavered. "The girl's
shard is the key. If we control it, we control the succession."

Aldric felt the weight of the Starfall Blade at his hip. He had carried it for
thirty years. Tonight it might finally earn its name.

"This isn't control, Leona. This is treason."

She smiled. It didn't reach her eyes.
