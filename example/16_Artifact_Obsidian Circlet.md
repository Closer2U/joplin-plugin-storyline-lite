# Note Title

```
[Artifact] Obsidian Circlet
```

# Note Body

```
---
storyline_codex: artifact
aliases: Circlet, The Dark Crown, Obsidian
power: Dominates weaker wills within line of sight
---

A circlet carved from a single piece of obsidian, set with fragments of a
shattered black diamond. It was created as a countermeasure to the Shattered
Crown — whoever wears it can resist the crown's influence and command those
who cannot.

**Current holder:** Unknown enemy commander during the siege of Thornfield.

**Weakness:** The circlet feeds on the wearer's life force. Extended use causes
premature aging and paranoia.
```
