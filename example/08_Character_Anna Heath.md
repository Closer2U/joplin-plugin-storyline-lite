Anna Heath grew up in the border villages, far from court politics. She was raised
by her grandmother after her parents disappeared during the Crown Wars.

She discovered the first shard of the Shattered Crown in the ruins of Kael's Tower
and has been hunted ever since.

**Personality:** Stubborn, compassionate, quietly brave. Tends to take on too much
responsibility. Has a dry sense of humor that surfaces at the worst moments.

**Appearance:** Dark hair, grey eyes, a scar across her left palm from the shard's
first pulse.
